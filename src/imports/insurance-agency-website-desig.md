Role: UI/UX Designer

Task: Design a high-converting, modern website for an Insurance Agency.

Style: Professional, trustworthy, clean, using a corporate color palette (e.g., Navy Blue, Emerald Green, or Deep Blue) with plenty of white space.

1. Header & Navigation Structure
Logo: Left side.

Menu Items with Mega-Menu/Dropdowns:

"Dla Ciebie i Rodziny": Dropdown containing: Majątek, Podróże, OC (with sub-links to Vehicles). Each sub-page must feature a "Card Layout" with a brief description: "What is it?", "What you get", "Recommended add-ons" (e.g., Voluntary AC info).

"Dla Firm i Pracowników": Similar structure to the individual section.

"Pomoc i Kontakt": Direct link to the footer/contact page.

CTA Button: "Zadzwoń teraz" in the top right corner.

2. Main Page Sections (In Order)
Hero Section: Big, emotional background image (family/business). Centered or left-aligned Motto. Large CTA button: "Sprawdź ofertę ubezpieczeniową".

Trust Bar (Counters): 3-column layout with animated numbers: [X]+ lat na rynku, [X]+ zadowolonych klientów, [X]+ lat doświadczenia.

Partners: A horizontal, grayscale slider with logos of collaborating insurance companies.

About Us: Split layout. Left: High-quality photo of the team/office. Right: Descriptive text about the company mission.

Value Propositions ("Dlaczego my"): 4-column grid with icons.

Indywidualne podejście: (one sentence description).

Troska o klienta: (one sentence description).

Kwalifikacje: (one sentence description).

Szybkość działania: (one sentence description).

Locations Section: Two distinct cards for two offices. Each includes: Address, Opening Hours, Google Maps pin icon.

Testimonials: A 5-star rating header with a horizontal slider/carousel for client reviews.

FAQ Section: Accordion style (5 expandable questions/answers).

3. Footer
Social Icons: FB, LinkedIn, Instagram.

Quick Links: Privacy Policy, Documents to Print (PDF icons), Terms of Service.

Newsletter/Contact info: Small contact snippet.

4. UI/UX Functionalities (Design Elements)
Floating Chat Widget: Bottom right corner. A "Need help?" bubble that expands into a lead form: "Our agent will contact you within 24h".

Calendar Integration UI: A "Book a Meeting" section with a toggle/dropdown to select Office A or Office B, integrated with a calendar view (synced with MS Office).

CMS Ready: Design clean, repeatable cards for easy content management.
